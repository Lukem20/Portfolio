Hello, I'm Luke.

I'm a frontend developer & web designer.

I create optimized, user-centered frontend interfaces that are responsive and elegant. I strive for WCAG AA accessibility and care deeply about the principals behind the a11y project. My skill set and education equip me with the resources to take on a variety of tasks that blend creativity with logical problem-solving.

I created my first website in 2019 and have enjoyed journey of learning ever since. I recently graduated from California State University, Monterey Bay with a BS from the School of Computing & Design studying Communication Design and a minor in Computer Science. I'm passionate about novel ways of expressing typography on the web and using graphics programming to play with interesting animations and visual effects.
